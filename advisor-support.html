Support & Help

Frequently Asked Questions

When do payouts process?
Earnings clear exactly 3 days after a session is successfully completed without disputes. Once cleared, you can withdraw them instantly.
How do I handle a no-show client?
If a client doesn't join within 10 minutes of the start time, you may mark the session as a "Client No-Show." You will still receive full compensation.
Can I change my hourly rate?
Yes, navigate to "Profile Settings" to update your rate. Note that this will only apply to future bookings, not existing scheduled sessions.
