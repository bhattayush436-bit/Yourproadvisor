Support & Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reschedule a session?
You can reschedule up to 24 hours before the session begins. Go to 'My Sessions', click on the upcoming meeting, and select 'Reschedule'.
How does the wallet system work?
You top up your wallet using a credit or debit card. When you book an advisor, the required amount is held in escrow and deducted only after the session successfully ends.
What happens if the advisor doesn't show up?
If an advisor misses a scheduled call without prior notice, you will receive a full automatic refund to your wallet within 24 hours.
