Support & Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reschedule a session?
You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your session starts by going to the "My Sessions" tab and clicking "Reschedule."
What happens if the advisor doesn't show up?
If your advisor is more than 10 minutes late, the session is canceled, and your wallet will be fully refunded instantly.
How do I get my code reviewed?
Once you book a session, you can use the "Messages" tab to securely send files or links (like Verilog code) to your advisor before the meeting.
